Straw Hat Pirates

Meet the legendary crew that will help Luffy become the King of Pirates!

Roronoa Zoro

Combatant (Swordsman)

The first member to join the crew. A master of the Three Sword Style (Santoryu) who aims to become the World's Greatest Swordsman. Known for his immense strength, loyalty, and terrible sense of direction.

Nami

Navigator

A talented navigator and cartographer who dreams of drawing a map of the entire world. She commands the weather using her Sorcery Clima-Tact and manages the crew's finances with an iron fist.

Usopp

Sniper

The son of Yasopp and a dreamer who aims to become a "Brave Warrior of the Sea". An expert marksman and inventor who uses his wits and an arsenal of unique gadgets (Pop Greens) to fight.

Vinsmoke Sanji

Cook

A master chef who dreams of finding the All Blue. He fights using only his legs to protect his hands for cooking. A chivalrous gentleman who never refuses a starving person a meal.

Tony Tony Chopper

Doctor

A reindeer who ate the Hito Hito no Mi (Human-Human Fruit). He dreams of becoming a doctor who can cure any disease. Despite his cute appearance, he is a powerful fighter with multiple transformation points.

Nico Robin

Archaeologist

The sole survivor of Ohara who can read Poneglyphs. She dreams of discovering the Rio Poneglyph and the true history of the world. She ate the Hana Hana no Mi, allowing her to sprout body parts anywhere.

Franky

Shipwright

A cyborg and genius shipwright who built the Thousand Sunny. He dreams of creating a ship of dreams that can sail to the end of the world. Powered by cola, he is super strong and durable!

Brook

Musician

A living skeleton who ate the Yomi Yomi no Mi (Revive-Revive Fruit). He dreams of reuniting with Laboon the whale. A master swordsman (Soul Solid) who can freeze enemies with the chill of the underworld.

Jinbe

Helmsman

The "Knight of the Sea" and a master of Fish-Man Karate. Former Warlord who dreams of coexistence between humans and fish-men. He provides wisdom, strength, and unmatched steering skills to the crew.
