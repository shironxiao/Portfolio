Monkey D. Luffy

The man who will become King of the Pirates! Luffy is a carefree young pirate with the power of the Gomu Gomu no Mi, which grants him a rubber body. His infectious optimism and unwavering determination inspire all who meet him.

"I'm gonna be King of the Pirates!"

Luffy's Journey

Luffy's journey across the Grand Line has been filled with incredible adventures, powerful enemies, and unbreakable friendships. From defeating the Warlords of the Sea to challenging the Emperors, Luffy has proven time and again that his will is unshakeable.

His bounty has grown from 30 million berries to over 3 billion berries, reflecting his growing influence and power. Each battle has pushed him to new heights, unlocking advanced techniques like Gear Second, Gear Third, Gear Fourth, and most recently, the awakened form of his Devil Fruit - Gear Fifth.

Gear Fifth

Gear Fifth is the awakened form of Luffy's Devil Fruit - the Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Nika. This ultimate transformation turns him into the legendary "Sun God" Nika, granting complete freedom in battle with cartoon-like abilities.

His hair and clothes turn white, his body emits steam, and he can manipulate his surroundings like rubber. This power makes him the "Warrior of Liberation" - strong enough to challenge the world's greatest fighters and prove he can become the Pirate King.
