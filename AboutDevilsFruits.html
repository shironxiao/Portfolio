Devil Fruits

Mysterious fruits that grant incredible powers at the cost of the ability to swim. Each fruit is unique and can only exist once in the world at a time! Those who consume them are cursed by the sea and will sink like a hammer in water. However, the extraordinary abilities they provide make them highly sought after by pirates and marines alike!

Legendary Devil Fruits

Mera Mera no Mi

The Flame-Flame Fruit

The Mera Mera no Mi is a Logia-type Devil Fruit that allows the user to create, control, and transform into fire at will, making them a Fire Human. This legendary fruit was once wielded by Portgas D. Ace, and later inherited by Sabo, Luffy's sworn brother.

Abilities: Complete fire manipulation, immunity to fire and heat, ability to fly using flames, intangibility against physical attacks, and devastating fire-based techniques like Fire Fist and Flame Emperor!

Ope Ope no Mi

The Op-Op Fruit

The Ope Ope no Mi is a Paramecia-type Devil Fruit that grants the ability to create a spherical territory where the user can manipulate anything within it. This fruit is wielded by Trafalgar D. Water Law, the Surgeon of Death, and is considered the "Ultimate Devil Fruit" worth 5 billion berries!

Abilities: Spatial manipulation within the ROOM, ability to swap objects and people, perform miraculous surgeries, cut anything without harming it, and even grant eternal youth through the Perennial Youth Operation (at the cost of the user's life)!
